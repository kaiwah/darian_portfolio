ABOUT ME

Hello, Darian Patterson here. Former dance performer turned web developer focused on crafting great web experiences with my sweet moves. In case you were wondering, Développé is a classical ballet term meaning "developing movement". Coding has been comparable to dance when it came to my understanding and learning. With dance, it all looks flawless when you see the final product of the dance, but what you don't see is all the hard work that went into what the audience gets to see and enjoy. That's the same mindset when I am developing a web page. I enjoy creating beautifully designed, intuitive and functional websites the same as I enjoy creating art through dance. For over the past 15 years, I have worked hard to improve my craft through dance and I experience so I understand that with web development, there is always room to learn. I take pride in playing a part in the audience's experience in any form and I look forward to providing the same for your company.
